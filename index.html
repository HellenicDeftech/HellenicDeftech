About

Hellenic DefTech 2025 is a premier technology and defense conference held on the 18th and 19th of October 2025, in Samos, Greece. It brings together experts, professionals, students, and innovators. The conference explores the fast-changing fields of technology, cybersecurity, AI, and modern defense systems.

Over the span of two impactful days, the event promotes open dialogue, collaboration, and innovation. It focuses on the intersection of national security, emerging technologies, and digital defense strategies. Attendees can participate in keynote speeches, hands-on workshops, live demonstrations, and expert panels. Sessions are led by top speakers from academia, industry, and military sectors.

Whether you're a student seeking insight from field leaders, a researcher pursuing breakthrough ideas, or a professional on the frontlines of innovation, Hellenic DefTech 2025 provides a powerful platform. You’ll find opportunities to learn, share, and build meaningful connections.

Explore cutting-edge topics in AI, cybersecurity, autonomous systems, and military innovation through expert-led sessions, networking opportunities, and interactive experiences at DefTech 2025.

Join us in shaping the future of tech and defense in the heart of the Aegean.
