Hellenic DefTech 2025

About

Hellenic DefTech 2025 is a premier technology and defense conference taking place on the 18th and 19th of October 2025, in Samos, Greece. Bringing together experts, professionals, students, and innovators, the conference explores the rapidly evolving fields of technology, cybersecurity, and defense.

These two days the event aims to foster dialogue, collaboration, and innovation at the intersection of national security, emerging technologies, and digital defense technologies. Attendees will have the opportunity to engage in keynote speeches, technical workshops, live demonstrations, and panel discussions led by distinguished speakers from academia, industry, and the armed forces.

Whether you're a student looking to learn from top experts, a researcher exploring the next breakthrough, or a professional working on the frontlines of defense technology, Hellenic DefTech 2025 offers a dynamic platform for sharing knowledge and networking.

Join us in shaping the future of tech and defense in the heart of the Aegean.
